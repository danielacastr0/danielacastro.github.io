About Me

I am from San Jose and I attend a small school.The type of dance that I am in is called folklorico. I have been dancing for about 9 years. The dresses are very heavy and sometimes we perform in fresno because of a festival that happens once a year. We take a class with people that come from pretty much anywhere. You get blisters bruises and it is very tiring when you dance for hours.
